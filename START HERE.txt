============================================================
  MoM Generator — Minutes of Meeting from a recording
  100% on your Mac. No cloud. No cost. Nothing leaves your laptop.
============================================================

WHAT IT DOES
  Drop in a meeting recording (Greek is fine) and it produces a clean Greek
  transcript that already labels WHO SAID WHAT — by reading the speaker names
  off the video (Google Meet / Teams). Download that transcript and hand it to
  Gemini (together with your screenshots) to generate the English Minutes of
  Meeting. Transcription runs on your Mac's GPU, so it's fast.
  (Optional: tick "also draft it locally" to get a rough English MoM offline.)

BEFORE YOU START — you need:
  • A Mac with Apple Silicon (M1/M2/M3/M4).
  • About 15 GB of free disk space.
  • Internet for the one-time install (downloads a few GB). Then it's offline.

------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 1 — INSTALL  (one time, ~10–15 min, no typing)
------------------------------------------------------------
  1) Unzip this folder somewhere easy, like your Desktop.

  2) Double-click:   «Install MoM Generator.command»

     • If macOS says "unidentified developer": right-click it (or Control-click)
       → Open → Open. You only do this once.
     • A window opens and does everything automatically. It may ask for your
       Mac password once. Just wait until it says "All set!".

  That's it — no commands to type, and no accounts or sign-ups needed.

------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 2 — USE IT  (every time)
------------------------------------------------------------
  1) Double-click:   «Start MoM Generator.command»
     (Same "right-click → Open" the first time, if asked.)
     A small window opens — keep it open while you work; close it when done.

  2) Your browser opens the tool. Type the attendees' names (recommended — it
     makes the speaker labelling rock-solid), drag in a recording
     (.mp4 .mov .m4a .mp3 .wav), then click "Generate transcript" and watch the
     progress bar. Speaker names are filled in from the video automatically;
     you can correct any of them and click "Apply names".

  3) When it's done, click "⬇️ Transcript for Gemini (.txt)" and attach that
     file to Gemini (with your screenshots) to write the MoM in English.
     A copy is also saved in:  Documents > MoM Outputs

------------------------------------------------------------
GOOD TO KNOW
------------------------------------------------------------
  • Keep the laptop lid OPEN while a meeting is processing (it pauses on sleep).
  • The first recording downloads the speech model (~3 GB) once, then it's fast.
  • Everything is local — your audio, transcript, and notes never leave the Mac.
  • Full details + troubleshooting are in README.md (in this folder).
